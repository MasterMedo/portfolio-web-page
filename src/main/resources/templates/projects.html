Stevil and Revi

Game made in Unity at college on the class 'Fundamentals of digital game development'. The game is co-op couch rouge-like 2 person platformer in which the first player controls the character and the second controls the map elements and fires rockets.

Lore

Stevil woke up to the sight of his home city collapsing. His work had already been almost destroyed. The only piece of equipment looking even half alive was Revi, his trusty smart home-robot, as well as the town security system head. In the attack that had just happened Revi's lost his legs. In all confusion of what's happening Stevil grabbed Revi by his broken wires and put him on his back as a backpack. The cities security system had been taken over by some enemies and Stevil's plan is to reach the city core and destroy them. Luckily, Revi still has some power over the city.

THREE image transition

Project made in CodePen by Szenia Zadvornykh. The animation is done by extending the THREE.js Mesh Phong Material (or Mesh Basic Material) to include arbitrary animation logic. This way the animation is done on the GPU, right in the browser.
